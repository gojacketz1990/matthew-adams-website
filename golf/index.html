Matthew's Journey to becoming a better golfer (again)!

Explore my path to improvement in my golf game starting in September of 2025. The one year goal is to get back down to a 5 handicap as I have skyrocketed to a 13 as of now.

Progress

September 21, 2025

First round yesterday, some progress. 81 - 37-44 so didn't finish very well.

Driving - mediocre still. Not really getting after the ball.

Ball Striking - a little better. Hit seven greens.

Short Game (Minus putting) - pretty good yesterday, couple really nice par saves, one chip in for birdie. Couiple that could have been saved but was off a bit.

Putting - good speed, good line yesterday. Made quite a few putts.

Today I am working on one of Hogan's pressure points of the upper left arm against the chest. Looking for more consistency.

Initial Diagnosis of Problems

Swing Diagnosis, Initial Goals, Game Plan.

What am I working on now?

Backswing - using the Smart Ball for a bunch of practice swing to try to keep the club in front of my body.

Downswing - using the Bryson dropping the club into your right pocket with your right hand to get the club in the slot.

Downswing - getting a better impact position with the left wrist angles correct and the grooves looking down at the ball. Getting a much better sound and feel now.

Current Handicap: 13.7

Initial Diagnosis of Problems

Swing Diagnosis, Initial Goals, Game Plan.

What is the problem?

Driving - mediocre, doing a lot of guiding the club instead of hitting it so losing distance. Get the driver stuck behind me at times, working on that with a smart ball.

Ball Striking - atrocious right now. Distance - bad, problem finding the low point of my swing, completely inconsistent.

Short Game (Minus putting) - Only thing keeping me competitive right now. Short game is pretty good, probably need some practice but not far off.

Putting - putting is pretty good right now, distance control is good. Could use a little more focus on lining putts up and not going off of memory.

Focus right now is on the backswing, keeping the club in front of me. Making a full turn and then keeping the club in front of me on the way down and through. Staying in posture.
